Last Bar

Last Bar is an indie dance and house inspired project based in Seattle. Inspired by the ethereal live sounds of acts like RUFUS Du Sol and Cut Copy and the driving rhythms of house music. Last Bar blends atmospheric vibes and reminiscent sounds to stir the emotions and feelings that great dance music can bring to you.

After launching his first remix of Hayden James' "Nowhere to Go" and subsequently a remix of The Knocks "Colors" which got great reception at the end of 2019, he plans release his first original tracks in 2020.
